Years in Sound is a mixed media installation by Erica Cheong. Six years of GitHub contribution data — 2021 to 2026 — are translated into music in real time. Each column (one week) becomes a chord. Contribution level maps to pitch: more activity, higher frequency. Empty days are silence.
